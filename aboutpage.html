About DataWipeX

DataWipeX is a tool that helps you completely remove data from storage devices like SSDs, hard drives, and USB drives. In today’s digital world, just deleting files or formatting a disk is not enough because the data can still be recovered by others.

Our tool makes sure the data is fully removed and cannot be recovered by using industry-standard wiping techniques. No matter if you are a single user, an IT manager, or a recycling company, DataWipeX gives you a quick and trusted way to keep your private data safe before you throw away, sell, or reuse a device.

Key Features:

  • One-click secure wipe
  • Support for SSDs, HDDs, and USB drives
  • Multiple erasure methods (overwrite, secure erase, NVMe sanitize)
  • Certificate of erasure for compliance & record-keeping
  • Simple web-based interface, no technical skills required

At DataWipeX, our mission is to make secure data disposal easy, transparent, and trustworthy.
